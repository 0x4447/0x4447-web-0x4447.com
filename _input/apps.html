Our Marketplace Apps

Regular consumers get beautiful, polished apps. We developers just get functionals and apps that often barely work. To improve on this, we decided to start building our own apps that would help us save time during our daily routine, and then release the apps to the world in the hope that others would find them as useful as we did.

QRCode Generator

This simple app generates QR Codes. It's nothing special, but it's set up according to the Apple Design Guidelines. This ensures that your eyes won't hurt as you use the app, which makes your entire experience much more enjoyable.

One-Time Password Manager

This one-time password solution is your best option for keeping online accounts safe, because even if someone does get hold of your password, they can't do anything with it unless they have your phone, too. Our OTP Manager gives you the flexibility you need to organize all of your sites and reduce the time you would otherwise spend looking for the right TOTP.
