About

0x4447 was create out of necessity after realizing that after all my years of experience I reached a point where I have the knowledge and the know how to pass this experience to others that are seeking this information. My goal with 0x4447 is to create a place where people will feel safe to make mistake, learn from them and become better, the same way I did it, with the difference that I’ll be there to help gracefully fail while helping understand the mistake faster and in a more meaningful way.

For whatever reason failing is seen as weakness and something unwanted, where truly failing is the only way for us humans to learn and become better.

Testing, running experiment and curiosity is built in to us, but the old generation created schools that remove with force this innate curiosity to wonder. I always fought back the grown ups and thankfully I kept my curiosity intact. I want to help bring the curiosity back out in people.

David Gatti - Owner
