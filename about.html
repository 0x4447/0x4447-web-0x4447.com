About

0x4447 was create out of necessity after realizing that after all my years of experience I reached a point where I have the knowledge and the know how to pass this experience to others that are seeking this information. My goal with 0x4447 is to create a place where people will feel safe to make mistake, learn from them and become better, the same way I did it, with the difference where I’ll be there to gracefully fail while helping understand the mistake faster and in a more meaningful way.

For whatever reason failing is seen as weakness and something unwanted, where truly failing is the only way for use humans to learn and become better. Testing, running experiment and curiosity is built in to us, and is been from us from a young age. I always rejected this idea and thankfully I kept my curiosity intact, and I want to bring it out from people.

David.
